About Spotify

Discover, manage, and share over 100 million tracks, including more than 5 million podcast titles and 350,000 audiobooks a la carte on Spotify.

Today, we are the world’s most popular audio streaming subscription service with a community of more than 574 million users, including 226 million Spotify Premium subscribers, across 184 markets.
